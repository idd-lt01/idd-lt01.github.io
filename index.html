The Program Leaders

Claire Hyeeun Son (Graduate 2018, Interaction Design and Development); Digital Media Designer, RBC

"The program and the research projects at George Brown helped me learn various aspects of interaction design and look at the problems from various angles. I have gained vast knowledge not only in interaction design but also in human-centered design. George Brown helped me break the shell and encouraged me to be a leader in finding better design solutions.”

Program Highlights • We place a strong emphasis on portfolio development and completion. For example, Semester 4 includes the creation of an immersive environment project that encourages outside-the-box thinking and is designed to test the limits of your understanding of interaction. • An optional field placement, arranged through the college, is available for eligible students with a 3.0+ GPA (that’s the equivalent of a B).* Speak to Program Co-ordinator for details. *Additional fees may apply.
